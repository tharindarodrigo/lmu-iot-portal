## 3.2 Executive Summary vs Introduction vs Conclusion

The video carefully differentiates these three sections.

### Executive Summary

* Complete summary of entire report.
* No references.
* No new information.
* Maximum one page.
* Not part of word count.
* Gives the full answer in brief .

### Introduction

* Explains what the report will cover.
* Does NOT include content details.
* No definitions or quotes.
* Part of word count.
* Previews structure only .

### Conclusion

* Summarizes what was covered.
* Restates final judgment.
* Includes final thoughts or key recommendations.
* Combines elements of summary and evaluation .
